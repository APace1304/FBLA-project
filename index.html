Habitat for Humanity




Habitat for Humanity is a nonprofit organization that works in more than 70 countries, as well as all 50 states. Habitat's vision is for everybody to have a decent place to live. To work toward this goal, Habitat partners with families in need who are willing to put in work for their future home and pay an affordable morgage plan.
